## Sold Out/Z-Reading

The **Sold Out/Z-Reading** feature is used to record the **daily sales summary** of the store.

This includes:

- Daily **gross sales**
    
- **Expenses** for the day
    
- **Tender amounts** (cash, other payment types)
    
- Uploading supporting images
    
- Sending the final record for syncing to **iDempiere**
    

This process is usually done **at the end of the day**.

---
